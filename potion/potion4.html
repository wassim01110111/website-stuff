Rules

  • You are finally about to become a True God! All that is left, is concocting this potion.
  • It takes equal measures of blue and red together to make your Apotheosis potion.
  • Any other ratio will make you lose control.
  • You must fill the entire eight-litre container with that mixture.
  • Be careful: you only have a of pours before the potion begins to bubble out of control.
  • Click a container to select it, then click another to pour. Clicking the same container again will deselect it. Overflow is handled naturally: any excess potion stays behind if the target container can't hold it all.
