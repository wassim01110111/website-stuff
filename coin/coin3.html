Rules

    • The Lord of Toilets is desperate, he has brought his stronger backer now, Audrey Hall. Beat them and you will be the richest person alive.
    • Objective: Remove all coins from the board except for one in moves.
    • You can move a coin by jumping it horizontally or vertically over an adjacent coin into an empty space. The jumped-over coin is removed.
    • You cannot jump over walls.
    • Coins that cannot move are not selectable.
    • Click a coin to see possible moves. Click again on a highlighted spot to move the coin.
    • Re-clicking a selected coin will deselect it.
    • The game ends when only one coin remains. If you achieve this, you win against the Lord of Toilets!

Hint 1
The coins on the outside will need to be moved toward the center.
Hint 2
As a general rule, try to maintain the horizontal symmetry of the coins as you progress. It should make the puzzle easier to understand.
Hint 3
Leave the top part of the puzzle until last. Start by removing the nine coins in the bottom half of the board. If you really need a hint to get started, the leftmost and rightmost coins in the third row from the bottom can be jumped over the balls above them.
Hint 4
Keep going until there are only two coins left in the bottom half. Make sure to keep the remaining coins as high up the board as possible. For the final move, the remaining two coins must be directly above the center space, so try to plan ahead.
